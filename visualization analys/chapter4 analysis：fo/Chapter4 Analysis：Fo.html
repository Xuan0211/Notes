Frame

These levels are nested（嵌套的）; the output from an upstream level above is input to the downstream level below. A block is the outcome of the design process at that level. The challenge of this nesting is that choosing the wrong block at an upstream level inevitably cascades to all downstream levels. If you make a poor choice in the abstraction stage, then even perfect choices at the idiom and algorithm levels will not result in a vis system that solves the intended problem.
