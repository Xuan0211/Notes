With a single key, separating into regions using that key yields one region per item.
区域通常以一维列表对齐方式排列，或水平或垂直。

视图本身覆盖了一个二维区域:对齐的项目列表横跨一个空间维度，显示值的区域横跨另一个空间维度。
The view itself covers a two-dimensional area: the aligned list of items stretches across one of the spatial dimensions, and the region in which the values are shown stretches across the other.

Dot and Line Charts

它们使用一个空间位置通道来表示数量属性，并使用另一个方向来表示第二个关键属性。
They use one spatial position channel to express a quantitative attribute, and use the other direction for a second key attribute.
不同之处在于，折线图还使用连接标记，通过显式地显示一个项目与下一个项目之间的关系来强调项目沿键轴的顺序。
The difference is that line charts also use connection marks to emphasize the ordering of the items along the key axis by explicitly showing the relationship between one item and the next.
因此，它们具有更强的趋势关系含义，使它们更适合于发现趋势的抽象任务。
Thus, they have a stronger implication of trend relationships, making them more suitable for the abstract task of spotting trends.

折线图应该用于有序键而不是分类键。
Line charts should be used for ordered keys but not categorical keys.
柱状图鼓励离散比较，而折线图鼓励趋势评估。
Bar charts encourage discrete comparisons, while line graphs encourage trend assessments.
一个优化：接近于45°的折线更有利于显示

The cluster heatmap idiom combines the basic heatmap with matrix reordering, where two attributes are reordered in combination.? The goal of matrix reordering is to group similar cells in order to check for largescale patterns between both attributes, just as the goal of reordering a single attribute is to see trends across a single one.
A cluster heatmap is the juxtaposed combination of a heatmap and two dendrograms showing the derived data of the cluster hierarchies used in the reodering.

Scatterplots are effective for the abstract tasks of providing overviews and characterizing distributions, and specifically for finding outliers and extreme values.
散点图对于判断两个属性之间相关性的抽象任务也非常有效。
Scatterplots are also highly effective for the abstract task of judging the correlation between two attributes.

散点图矩阵是一个矩阵，其中每个单元格包含一个完整的散点图。
A SPLOM shows all possible pairwise combinations of attributes, with the original attributes as the rows and columns.
SPLOMs大量用于寻找相关性、趋势和离群值的抽象任务，与它们组成的散点图组件的使用保持一致。
SPLOMs are heavily used for the abstract tasks of finding correlations, trends, and outliers, in keeping with the usage of their constituent scatterplot components.

Parallel Coordinates

并行坐标是一种使用空间位置同时可视化许多数量属性的方法。
The idiom of parallel coordinates is an approach for visualizing many quantitative attributes at once using spatial position.
在散点图中，项目是用点表示的，而在平行坐标中，单个项目是用锯齿线表示的，该锯齿线穿过平行轴，在相关属性的项目值的位置上只穿过一次轴。
While an item is shown with a dot in a scatterplot, with parallel coordinates a single item is represented by a jagged line that zigzags through the parallel axes, crossing each axis exactly once at the location of the item’s value for the associated attribute
应用范围
  1. 相关性。
    One original motivation by the designers of parallel coordinates was that they can be used for the abstract task of checking for correlation between attributes.
    平行坐标被设计用来显示相邻轴之间的相关性。
    Parallel coordinates were designed to show correlation between neighboring axes.
    • 在顶部，平行线显示完全正相关。
      At the top, parallel lines show perfect positive correlation.
    • 在底部，所有的线在两个轴之间的一个点交叉，显示出完美的负相关。
      At the bottom, all of the lines cross over each other at a single spot in between the two axes, showing perfect negative correlation.
    • 在中间，交叉的混合显示不相关的数据。
      In the middle, the mix of crossings shows uncorrelated data.
  2. 对所有属性的概述、查找单个属性的范围、选择项目的范围以及异常值检测。
    Parallel coordinates are more often used for other tasks, including overview over all attributes, finding the range of individual attributes, selecting a range of items, and outlier detection
    例如，在图7.14(a)中，第三个轴(标记为manu wrkrs)的值的范围很广，接近标准化极限628.50和441.50，而第6个轴(标记为cleared)的值的范围更窄;第四个轴上的顶部项目，标记为handgun lc，似乎是该属性的一个异常值。
    For example, in Figure 7.14(a), the third axis, labeled manu wrkrs, has a broad range nearly to the normalized limits of 628.50 and 441.50, whereas the range of values on the sixth axis, labeled cleared, is more narrow; the top item on the fourth axis, labeled handgun lc, appears to be an outlier with respect to that attribute.
