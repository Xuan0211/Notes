Tasks

A task is a collection of related Activities.
It is capable of spanning multiple processes. 它能够跨越多个进程。
•Affinities
A naffinity means a preference for each activity to belong to a certain task. naffinity表示对每个活动属于某个任务的偏好。
An individual affinity can be set for each activity: 可以为每个活动设置单独的affinity:
By default, a new activity is launched into the task of the activity that called startActivity(). 默认情况下，将在称为startActivity() 的活动的任务中启动一个新活动。
